About the Project

About

MSU Archaeology Collections is a project developed by Zachary Francis-Hapner as the result of the 2019-2020 Cultural Heritage Informatics Fellowship. The purpose of this project is to make the archaeological collections being held by the anthropology department more open accessible to the public.
